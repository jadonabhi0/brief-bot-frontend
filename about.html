About BriefBot

Welcome to BriefBot, your go-to Chrome extension for quick and efficient web page summarization. With BriefBot, you can extract the key content and main points from web articles, blog posts, and news pages, allowing you to get the list of the information without reading through lengthy texts.

Features

  • Summarize web pages to get concise overviews
  • Toggle between dark and light themes for comfortable reading
  • Generate both full summaries and key sentence summaries
  • Copy summaries to your clipboard for easy sharing
  • Provides estimated reading time for each page
  • Displays word count and character count of content
